OCHA Basic / Colours

Color scheme based on the OCHA palette, see OCHA Graphics Style Book (pdf)

Use the contextual colours (text, background, border) rather than the palette colours directly.

Use a colour contrast checker to confirm that your colour combinations are accessible - http://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/.

Sass file: variables/_colours.scss

Colour palette

  • $ocha-blue: #026CB6;
  • $dark-blue: #002D80;
  • $light-grey: #F2F2F2;
  • $mid-light-grey: #CCCCCC;
  • $mid-grey: #595959;
  • $red: #BF0013;

Background colours

  • $light-bg-color: $light-grey;
  • $primary-bg-color: $ocha-blue;
  • $secondary-bg-color: $dark-blue;

Border colours

  • $border-color: $mid-light-grey;
  • $highlight-border-color: $dark-blue;
  • $error-border-color: $red;
  • $light-border-color: $light-grey;

Text colours

  • $body-text-color: $mid-grey;
  • $heading-text-color: $ocha-blue;
  • $secondary-heading-text-color: $dark-blue;
  • $light-text-color: white;
  • $link-text-color: $dark-blue;
  • $active-link-text-color: $ocha-blue;
  • $error-text-color: $red;
